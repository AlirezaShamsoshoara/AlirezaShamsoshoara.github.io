## [AI Daily Paper Summary](/blog/ai-papers/)

I read AI and ML research papers daily and post concise summaries here. Each entry covers the key contributions, methods, and takeaways from recent papers across topics like deep learning, reinforcement learning, NLP, computer vision, and more.

<!-- LATEST_PAPERS_START -->
**Latest — May 25, 2026**

1. **SkillOpt: Executive Strategy for Self-Evolving Agent Skills** — First systematic text-space optimizer for agent skills; achieves best or tied results on all 52 evaluated cells across 6 benchmarks and 7 models. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.23904)
2. **Lens: Rethinking Training Efficiency for Foundational Text-to-Image Models** — 3.8B-parameter T2I model matching 6B+ SOTA with only ~19.3% of the training compute of Z-Image. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21573)
3. **Rethinking Cross-Layer Information Routing in Diffusion Transformers** — Systematic empirical analysis of cross-layer information routing in DiTs, proposing improved mechanisms beyond inherited residual connections. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20708)
4. **SciAtlas: A Large-Scale Knowledge Graph for Automated Scientific Research** — Knowledge graph enabling topological multi-hop reasoning over scientific literature for agentic research workflows. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.22878)
5. **StepAudio 2.5 Technical Report** — Unified audio-language foundation model matching specialized baselines across ASR, TTS, and real-time spoken interaction. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.23463)
6. **SWIM: Aligning Vision and Language for Video Fine-grained Object Understanding** — Training strategy enabling fine-grained video object understanding from text prompts alone — no visual prompts at inference. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.18018)
7. **From Raw Experience to Skill Consumption: A Systematic Study of Model-Generated Agent Skills** — First end-to-end study of skill lifecycle (extraction → retrieval → consumption) with unified SkillLens evaluation framework. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.23899)
8. **PhotoFlow: Agentic 3D Virtual Photography Missions** — Agent that infers shots, selects camera parameters, and renders photographs given only a 3D scene and a language intent. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.23771)
9. **VGenST-Bench: A Benchmark for Spatio-Temporal Reasoning via Active Video Synthesis** — New MLLM benchmark using generative video synthesis for precise, controllable spatio-temporal reasoning evaluation. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.22570)
10. **PiD: Fast and High-Resolution Latent Decoding with Pixel Diffusion** — Replaces VAE decoder in T2I systems with a pixel diffusion model for sharper megapixel-scale generation without proportional compute overhead. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.23902)
