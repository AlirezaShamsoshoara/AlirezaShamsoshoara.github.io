## [AI Daily Paper Summary](/blog/ai-papers/)

I read AI and ML research papers daily and post concise summaries here. Each entry covers the key contributions, methods, and takeaways from recent papers across topics like deep learning, reinforcement learning, NLP, computer vision, and more.

<!-- LATEST_PAPERS_START -->
**Latest — June 09, 2026**

1. **SWE-Explore** — Isolates repository exploration as a standalone skill for coding agents, benchmarking 848 issues across 10 languages and 203 repos to reveal where agentic explorers outperform classical retrieval. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07297)
2. **On the Geometry of On-Policy Distillation** — First geometric characterization of OPD training dynamics, revealing "subspace locking" where updates collapse into a narrow low-dimensional channel. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07082)
3. **LatentSkill** — Converts textual agent skills into LoRA adapters via a hypernetwork, improving ALFWorld success by 21.4 pts while using 64.1% fewer prefill tokens. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.06087)
4. **Latent Spatial Memory for Video World Models** — Stores 3D scene memory in diffusion latent space, achieving 10.57× faster video generation and 55× memory reduction over explicit 3D baselines. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.09828)
5. **CoVEBench** — Benchmark exposing that video editing models fail at compositional multi-edit instructions, with 416 videos and 9,990 fine-grained checklist items. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.08415)
6. **FlashMemory-DeepSeek-V4** — Lookahead Sparse Attention enables lightning-fast KV cache indexing for ultra-long context serving without quality degradation. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.09079)
7. **SpatialWorld** — Evaluates MLLMs on interactive 3D spatial reasoning in real-world scenarios, revealing significant gaps in planning and acting in physical space. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.09669)
8. **Human Psychometric Questionnaires Mischaracterize LLM Behavior** — Shows that personality questionnaire results don't predict actual LLM conversational behavior across 8 open-source models. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.10078)
9. **Echo-Memory** — Controlled ablation study of memory architectures in action-conditioned world models for multi-segment video generation. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.09803)
10. **Reasoning Arena** — Trace tournaments as a reward signal for RLVR on open-ended reasoning tasks where verifiable rewards are unavailable. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.09380)
