## [AI Daily Paper Summary](/blog/ai-papers/)

I read AI and ML research papers daily and post concise summaries here. Each entry covers the key contributions, methods, and takeaways from recent papers across topics like deep learning, reinforcement learning, NLP, computer vision, and more.

<!-- LATEST_PAPERS_START -->
**Latest — May 07, 2026**

1. **MiniCPM-o 4.5** — Real-time full-duplex omni-modal interaction (audio + video + text simultaneously), rivaling Gemini 2.5 Flash at a fraction of the compute cost. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.27393)
2. **Stream-T1** — Applies test-time scaling to streaming video generation using temporal coherence as a guidance signal, improving quality without retraining. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.04461)
3. **OpenSearch-VL** — Fully open recipe for training frontier multimodal search agents with data, trajectory synthesis pipelines, and training procedures released. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05185)
4. **When to Think, When to Speak** — Side-by-Side Interleaving separates internal chain-of-thought from streamed output, resolving the latency vs. reasoning quality trade-off. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.03314)
5. **RLDX-1** — Vision-Language-Action model for dexterous robot manipulation with motion awareness, memory, and goal-conditioned generalization via RL. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.03269)
6. **Stream-R1** — Reliability-perplexity aware reward distillation for video diffusion that selectively emphasizes high-signal supervision frames. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.03849)
7. **JoyAI-Image** — Unified MLLM + MMDiT architecture enabling visual understanding, text-to-image generation, and image editing with spatial intelligence. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.04128)
8. **The First Token Knows** — Single-decode confidence from the first generated token predicts hallucination risk at near-zero overhead in production LLMs. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05166)
9. **CreativityBench** — New benchmark revealing that LLMs strong at conventional reasoning struggle with affordance-based creative tool repurposing. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.02910)
10. **Rethinking Reasoning-Intensive Retrieval** — New benchmarks and training for retrievers in agentic pipelines where topical similarity doesn't capture reasoning needs. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.04018)
