## [AI Daily Paper Summary](/blog/ai-papers/)

I read AI and ML research papers daily and post concise summaries here. Each entry covers the key contributions, methods, and takeaways from recent papers across topics like deep learning, reinforcement learning, NLP, computer vision, and more.

<!-- LATEST_PAPERS_START -->
**Latest — May 24, 2026**

1. **DelTA: Discriminative Token Credit Assignment for RLVR** — Fine-grained token-level credit assignment for RLVR; treats policy-gradient updates as linear discriminators over token gradients to improve LLM reasoning. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21467)
2. **TransitLM: Map-Free Transit Route Generation** — 13M+ route records from 4 Chinese cities; trains LLMs for public transit navigation without any map infrastructure. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.22355)
3. **Perception or Prejudice: MLLMs and Personality Reasoning** — New GPR benchmark shows MLLMs rely on visual stereotypes rather than behavioral evidence when inferring personality traits. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.22109)
4. **π-Bench: Proactive Personal Assistant Agent Evaluation** — Benchmark for long-horizon, underspecified workflows; frontier agents fail significantly at proactive, multi-step assistance. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.14678)
5. **Full Attention Strikes Back: Dense-to-Sparse in ~100 Steps** — Dense LLMs converted to sparse attention in ~100 fine-tuning steps with no accuracy loss, slashing long-context inference cost. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.16928)
6. **ACC: Compiling Agent Trajectories for Long-Context Training** — Repurposes agent tool-call trajectories as long-context training data, improving LLM long-context reasoning without costly curation. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21850)
7. **PhysX-Omni: Unified Physical 3D Generation** — First framework generating simulation-ready 3D assets (rigid, deformable, articulated) with physically accurate properties in one system. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21572)
8. **LatentOmni: Audio-Visual Latent Reasoning for MLLMs** — Bypasses text-CoT lossy compression by reasoning directly in a unified audio-visual latent space for omni-modal understanding. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.22012)
9. **SEGA: Spectral-Energy Guided Attention for DiT Resolution Extrapolation** — Training-free high-resolution generation beyond DiT training range via frequency-specific spectral-energy attention scaling. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.22668)
10. **Forecasting Scientific Progress with AI (CUSP)** — Benchmark testing AI's ability to anticipate novel discoveries under knowledge cutoffs; current models fall well short of genuine forecasting. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.22681)
