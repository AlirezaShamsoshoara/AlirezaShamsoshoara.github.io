## [AI Daily Paper Summary](/blog/ai-papers/)

I read AI and ML research papers daily and post concise summaries here. Each entry covers the key contributions, methods, and takeaways from recent papers across topics like deep learning, reinforcement learning, NLP, computer vision, and more.

**Latest — May 09, 2026**

1. **AI Co-Mathematician** — Google DeepMind agentic workbench for open-ended math research; sets new high score on FrontierMath benchmark. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06651)
2. **EMO (AllenAI)** — MoE pretraining objective that yields modular, deployable specialist sub-models without sacrificing general capability. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06663)
3. **Prescriptive Scaling Laws** — Actionable compute allocation rules for data-constrained LLM training, telling you exactly when and how much to repeat data. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.01640)
4. **TIDE** — Re-injects token identity at every Transformer layer to mitigate the Rare Token Problem and improve long-context understanding. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06216)
5. **Balanced Aggregation (GRPO fix)** — Identifies and corrects aggregation bias in GRPO group reward computation, improving reasoning model training stability. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.04077)
6. **Think, then Score** — Decouples reasoning from scoring in video reward models, yielding sharper rewards for generative video post-training. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05922)
7. **Verifier-Backed Problem Generation** — Uses formal verifiers to synthesize genuinely hard, valid math problems at scale for reasoning model curricula. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06660)
8. **Positive-Only Policy Optimization** — Derives implicit negative gradient signal from positive rollouts alone, cutting RLVR sample costs without hurting performance. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06650)
9. **STALE** — Benchmark for evaluating whether LLM agents can detect and revise stale memories when new observations contradict stored beliefs. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06527)
10. **Cited but Not Verified** — Systematic framework for evaluating source attribution fidelity in LLM deep research agents; finds alarming miscitation rates. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06635)
