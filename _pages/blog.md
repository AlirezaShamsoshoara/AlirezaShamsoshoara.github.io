## [AI Daily Paper Summary](/blog/ai-papers/)

I read AI and ML research papers daily and post concise summaries here. Each entry covers the key contributions, methods, and takeaways from recent papers across topics like deep learning, reinforcement learning, NLP, computer vision, and more.

<!-- LATEST_PAPERS_START -->
**Latest — May 16, 2026**

1. **Lighthouse Attention** — Training-only hierarchical attention for extreme sequence lengths; NousResearch open-sourced a two-stage recipe that beats vanilla full attention loss. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06554)
2. **VGGT-Edit** — Text-conditioned 3D scene editing via residual field prediction in a single feed-forward pass, avoiding multi-view inconsistency. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.15186)
3. **PanoWorld** — Spherical spatial cross-attention for 360° panoramic MLLMs, enabling full-surround spatial reasoning without perspective decomposition. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.13169)
4. **Orchard** — Open-source agentic LLM training framework from Microsoft Research covering coding agents, GUI nav, and personal assistant tasks. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.15040)
5. **ViMU** — First benchmark for metaphorical video understanding, revealing VLMs excel at literal comprehension but fail at figurative/cultural subtext. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.14607)
6. **Forcing-KV** — Hybrid KV cache compression for AR video diffusion that categorizes heads as static or dynamic to cut memory without losing coherence. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.09681)
7. **RAVEN** — Real-time autoregressive video extrapolation using CM-GRPO to close the train-inference distribution gap for long-horizon generation. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.15190)
8. **CurveBench** — Exact topological reasoning benchmark over nested Jordan curves; most VLMs fail, exposing heuristic spatial reasoning. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.14068)
9. **BOOKMARKS** — Search-based storyline memory for role-playing agents that preserves fine-grained character detail over long multi-session contexts. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.14169)
10. **WildTableBench** — 402 real-world table images with 928 QA pairs; only 1 of 21 frontier models exceeds 50% accuracy on messy in-the-wild tables. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.01018)
