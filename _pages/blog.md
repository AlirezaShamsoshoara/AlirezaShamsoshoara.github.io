## [AI Daily Paper Summary](/blog/ai-papers/)

I read AI and ML research papers daily and post concise summaries here. Each entry covers the key contributions, methods, and takeaways from recent papers across topics like deep learning, reinforcement learning, NLP, computer vision, and more.

<!-- LATEST_PAPERS_START -->
**Latest — May 31, 2026**

1. **AgentDoG 1.5: A Lightweight and Scalable Alignment Framework for AI Agent Safety and Security** — Taxonomy-guided safety alignment for open-world agents, training 0.8B–8B models on ~1k samples to match frontier closed-source performance with two-orders-of-magnitude lower environment overhead. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29801)
2. **OmniRetrieval: Unified Retrieval across Heterogeneous Knowledge Sources** — Single NL-query interface spanning text, tables, knowledge graphs, and property graphs, dispatching source-native queries without collapsing structural distinctions; beats single-source baselines across 13 datasets. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29250)
3. **CollectionLoRA: Collecting 50 Effects in 1 LoRA via Multi-Teacher On-Policy Distillation** — Compresses up to 50 effect LoRAs into one via multi-teacher on-policy distillation, eliminating concept bleeding and style degradation from cascaded LoRA pipelines. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.25378)
4. **minWM: A Full-Stack Open-Source Framework for Real-Time Interactive Video World Models** — End-to-end pipeline converting T2V/TI2V foundation models into camera-controllable few-step autoregressive world models with full data, training, and distillation recipes. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30263)
5. **YoCausal: How Far is Video Generation from World Model? A Causality Perspective** — VoE-inspired benchmark using temporally reversed videos as counterfactuals; shows 13 state-of-the-art VDMs perceive the arrow of time without genuinely understanding causality. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30346)
6. **GenClaw: Code-Driven Agentic Image Generation** — Agentic paradigm where code (SVG, HTML, Three.js) serves as a controllable intermediate canvas between linguistic reasoning and pixel synthesis, replacing the black-box prompt-rewrite loop. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30248)
7. **EarlyTom: Early Token Compression Completes Fast Video Understanding** — Training-free token compression inside the vision encoder, achieving 2.65× TTFT reduction and 61% FLOPs reduction on LLaVA-OneVision-7B with no accuracy drop. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30010)
8. **UniSteer: Text-Guided Flow Matching in Activation Space for Versatile LLM Steering** — Flow matching over LLM residual-stream activations conditioned on natural language, unifying behavioral control, truthfulness, concept steering, and multi-constraint following in one model. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30076)
9. **Skill0.5: Joint Skill Internalization and Utilization for Out-of-Distribution Generalization in Agentic RL** — Hybrid agentic RL combining privileged distillation for general skill internalization with a difficulty-aware dynamic router for task-specific utilization; improves OOD generalization on ALFWorld and WebShop. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.28424)
10. **LoMo: Local Modality Substitution for Deeper Vision-Language Fusion** — Data curation paradigm that reformulates single-modality prompts into interleaved multimodal sequences, fixing "carrier sensitivity" in VLMs and boosting multiple benchmarks by 2.67–2.82 points. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30265)
