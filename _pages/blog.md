## [AI Daily Paper Summary](/blog/ai-papers/)

I read AI and ML research papers daily and post concise summaries here. Each entry covers the key contributions, methods, and takeaways from recent papers across topics like deep learning, reinforcement learning, NLP, computer vision, and more.

<!-- LATEST_PAPERS_START -->
**Latest — May 08, 2026**

1. **Skill1** — Single RL policy that jointly learns to select, use, and distill skills into a persistent library from one task-outcome signal; tops HF daily board. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06130)
2. **Cola DLM** — Hierarchical latent diffusion LM separating global semantics from local text realization, with strong scaling curves vs. AR and LLaDA baselines. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06548)
3. **MiA-Signature** — Compact global-activation representation for long-context understanding inspired by cognitive "global ignition"; improves RAG and agentic benchmarks. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06416)
4. **Meno & Friends (SemEval-2026 Winner)** — Judge-orchestrated 7-LLM ensemble that won 1st/26 teams on MTRAGEval Task B with a 7B-class model beating much larger baselines. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.04523)
5. **MARBLE** — Multi-reward RLHF for diffusion models via QP-based gradient harmonization; improves all five reward dimensions simultaneously on SD3.5 Medium. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06507)
6. **FFDC (WAMs)** — Lightweight verifier that decides when to trust world model rollouts vs. replan; cuts WAM forward passes 69.1% and boosts real-world robot success 35%. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06222)
7. **Direct Corpus Interaction (DCI)** — Agentic search via grep/file-reads on raw corpora beats tuned vector retrieval on BRIGHT and BEIR — no indexing required. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05242)
8. **CDM** — Continuous-time diffusion distillation extending DMD to arbitrary trajectory points; competitive few-step visual fidelity without GANs, code released. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06376)
9. **LoPE** — Prepending Lorem Ipsum to prompts unlocks alternative reasoning paths in GRPO training, solving the zero-advantage problem on hard questions. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05566)
10. **AVI Survey** — First comprehensive survey of Audio-Visual Intelligence via large foundation models, covering understanding, generation, and interaction with a unified taxonomy. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.04045)
