## [AI Daily Paper Summary](/blog/ai-papers/)

I read AI and ML research papers daily and post concise summaries here. Each entry covers the key contributions, methods, and takeaways from recent papers across topics like deep learning, reinforcement learning, NLP, computer vision, and more.

<!-- LATEST_PAPERS_START -->
**Latest — June 18, 2026**

1. **RNG-Bench** — Evaluates whether multimodal LLMs can act on observations no longer visible, using Matching Pairs and 3D Maze games with controlled difficulty axes. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.19338)
2. **Kairos** — World model stack for Physical AI with native knowledge acquisition, long-horizon state persistence, and efficient real-world deployment. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.16533)
3. **Guava** — Universal tool-use harness for embodied manipulation combining LLM reasoning with modular perception, planning, and control modules. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.18363)
4. **EfficientRollout** — System-aware self-speculative decoding that significantly speeds up RL rollout throughput without sacrificing policy quality. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.18967)
5. **SAE Interventions are Unreliable** — Shows that SAE feature clamping fails to suppress LLM behaviors; suppressed behaviors recover through other pathways. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.18322)
6. **Dual-Path Spatial VLMs** — RL approach training spatial VLMs to dynamically select between linguistic CoT and explicit 3D grounding per query. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17539)
7. **Trust the Right Teacher** — Quality-aware on-policy self-distillation for GUI grounding that avoids degradation on coordinate-sensitive tasks. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.18101)
8. **LLM-as-Environment-Engineer** — Policy model analyzes its own failures to automatically design the next RL training environment for multi-agent reasoning. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17682)
9. **OmniAgent** — Omni-modal agent treating active perception as reasoning, dynamically selecting which video frames to attend to for efficient long-video understanding. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.19341)
10. **STARE** — Surprisal-guided per-token advantage reweighting that prevents entropy collapse in GRPO-style RL training of reasoning models. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.19236)
