## [AI Daily Paper Summary](/blog/ai-papers/)

I read AI and ML research papers daily and post concise summaries here. Each entry covers the key contributions, methods, and takeaways from recent papers across topics like deep learning, reinforcement learning, NLP, computer vision, and more.

**Latest — July 14, 2026**

1. **Weak-to-Strong Generalization via Direct On-Policy Distillation** — Direct-OPD reuses a small teacher's RL policy shift as a dense reward signal to lift a stronger student, boosting Qwen3-1.7B from 48.3% to 58.3% on AIME 2024. [arxiv.org/abs/2607.05394](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.05394)
2. **ABot-N1** — A visual-language navigation foundation model that decouples slow VLM reasoning from a fast action expert, lifting urban POI arrival rate to 77.3%. [arxiv.org/abs/2607.10383](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.10383)
3. **ABot-AgentOS** — A general robotic "Agent OS" with lifelong multi-modal graph memory and a failure-driven self-evolution loop. [arxiv.org/abs/2607.10350](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.10350)
4. **ABot-3DWorld 0** — A universal 3D world model that turns text/image/video into an explorable 3D Gaussian Splatting scene via a compact spatial-generative primitive. [arxiv.org/abs/2607.11673](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.11673)
5. **StudioRecon** — Reconstructs dynamic 4D human-scene captures from sparse, low-overlap camera rigs using video diffusion plus deformable Gaussians. [arxiv.org/abs/2607.09125](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.09125)
6. **LightMem-Ego** — An open-source streaming multimodal memory system for wearables/AI-glasses that tiers egocentric audio/video into current/short/long-term memory. [arxiv.org/abs/2607.11487](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.11487)
7. **AdvancedMathBench** — A doctoral-level proof generation and verification benchmark where even GPT-5.5-xhigh scores only 75.8/66.1, showing big headroom remains. [arxiv.org/abs/2607.11849](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.11849)
8. **Metacognition in LLMs** — A comprehensive survey of methods, benchmarks, and open questions in LLM self-awareness and confidence calibration. [arxiv.org/abs/2607.11881](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.11881)
9. **Inside the Unfair Judge** — Mechanistic study finding LLM-as-judge bias lives in a steerable, low-dimensional subspace of hidden states. [arxiv.org/abs/2607.11871](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.11871)
10. **EgoSteer** — A full-stack VLA system pretrained on 9.6K hours of egocentric video, executing 40+ dexterous manipulation tasks with human-in-the-loop correction. [arxiv.org/abs/2607.09701](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.09701)
