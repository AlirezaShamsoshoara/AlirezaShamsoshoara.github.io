## [AI Daily Paper Summary](/blog/ai-papers/)

I read AI and ML research papers daily and post concise summaries here. Each entry covers the key contributions, methods, and takeaways from recent papers across topics like deep learning, reinforcement learning, NLP, computer vision, and more.

<!-- LATEST_PAPERS_START -->
**Latest — June 17, 2026**

1. **UniAR** — Unified multimodal autoregressive model with a single discrete visual tokenizer for both image understanding and generation; state-of-the-art on generation and editing benchmarks. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.18249)
2. **Variable-Width Transformers** — Nonuniform layer-width allocation achieves better performance-per-parameter by concentrating capacity where each layer needs it most. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.18246)
3. **ActWorld** — Interactive world model with action-aware memory that supports rich object interactions (pick up, open, physics triggers) beyond navigation-only predecessors. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17730)
4. **Text-Vision Co-Instructed Image Editing** — Fuses text semantics and spatial visual prompts simultaneously for editing that is both semantically expressive and spatially precise. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.16767)
5. **ZoPPO** — Embeds teacher guidance as prompts rather than gradient targets, defining a zone of proximal development for stable distillation-RL hybrid training. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.18216)
6. **ACE-Ego-0** — VLA pretrained on both egocentric human video and robot trajectories, bridging data scarcity in robotic manipulation via abundant human egocentric data. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17200)
7. **GameCraft-Bench** — Benchmark for end-to-end playable game generation in a real engine from natural-language specs, testing multi-file dependency and asset management. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17861)
8. **ChLogic** — English-Chinese aligned logical reasoning benchmark revealing significant cross-lingual degradation in LLMs despite strong English performance. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17905)
9. **Aligning Quantum Operators with LLMs** — Maps unitary quantum operators into LLM latent space, enabling language models to reason about Clifford+T circuit synthesis tasks. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.13811)
10. **LoopCoder-v2** — Parallel Loop Transformers achieve multi-loop representational depth at single-pass latency via cross-loop position offsets and shared-KV gated attention. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.18023)
